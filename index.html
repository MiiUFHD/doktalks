About DOK Talks

This conference is for enriching women's lives by talking about feelings that sometimes can be hard to express and to have a deeper connection between all women.Our goal is to create a community trust within women.Where God can take us knowing that we can be open,honest,and unified somewhere to be vulnerable.The fearless female's journey is not without fear - it is about leaping into the fear,embracing change and becoming stronger through every challenge.Our goal is to inspire and be inspired and to light someone's candle along the way.

Speakers

Donna Sutton

Donna Sutton is our dedicated pastor’s wife. She takes time to emotionally invest in her and her husband’s congregation, New Life Church in Birmingham, AL. Moving from Louisiana to Birmingham, AL in 1987 with three small children, Sis. Sutton knows all too well the many roles a woman, wife and mother play within a family. She is a phenomenal example of how a woman of God should behave in all circumstances. Other than ministering alongside her husband, Pastor Barry Sutton, she loves sweets, traveling and spending time with her grandchildren.

Jessica Gibson

Jessica Gibson is a dynamic worship leader and teacher. Her passion for children and education is manifested through her exemplary work on the administrative teams for both New Life Academy and our Sunday School department. When she’s not devoting time to childhood development, she loves to yard sale, spend time with her husband, Davin, and make memories with her two young sons, Urijah and Liam.

Ashley Collins

Ashley Collins works alongside her husband, Stephen Collins, as Youth Directors. Her gentle, quiet spirit causes many to seek her out whenever they need advice or just a listening ear. Her love and sincere devotion to God rings clear every time she ministers in song or music. In her spare time, she enjoys reading, writing God-inspired music, and spending time with her husband and two children, Taylor and Ashton.

FAQ's

Will there be childcare available?

While families are highly important to us,there will not be childcare provided this year unless you are a single mom.Please make proper arrangments for childcare at that time.WE DO however,encourage atendees to ask husbands or trustworthy people you know to watch your children.

Do I have to register now or can I register at the door?

We prepare food arrangements,DOK GOODY BAGS,and a capacity of classes in advance. Knowing that,we recommend you to register early.However,WE DO plan for same - day registers but may not garuntee full expirience.

May any ladies attend?

Absolutely! As long your 11 and up.Everyone who wants to grow their faith and unity may attend DOK Talks. Not only do we want you to come, but we hope you'll bring friends.

Can I transfer my registration to another person?

Yes! Please email your name, the name of the person taking your registration and their email address to (insert button here). Please note that we are unable to give refunds but will help you transfer your registration as best we can.

Are scholarships available?

We want to make sure that every woman who wants to attend DOK Talks is able to. That’s why we offer scholarships for those who might not be able to afford tickets themselves. Our ability to do this depends on the generosity of others – we hope that everyone who is able would consider making a donation to help provide a scholarship for someone else. For more information on how to apply for a scholarship or make a donation, please click (insert button) .

Will there be breakouts at DOK Talks this year?

Yes, there will be optional breakouts throughout the conference. We will run each breakout twice so you will be able to attend several. This year, the breakout will be held in the Developmental Growth and Youth wing.

On Saturday at 1:20 PM Lunch will be served in the GYM.Water bottles and a snack bar will be in your goodie bag;which will be available at the time of check in.
